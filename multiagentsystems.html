About Multi-Agent Systems

A multi-agent system is a team of AI agents, each with a defined role, that work independently yet collaboratively to solve parts of a larger task. Agents can hand off work, coordinate actions in sequence or in parallel, share context, and collectively achieve goals more efficiently than a single agent alone.
