About the AI Data Launchpad Program

AI Data Launchpad is a structured program to enable your move from learning to execution.

  • Instructor-led, hands-on workshop on advanced technologies (e.g., multi-agent autonomous systems)
  • Ideation to identify practical AI use-cases for your company
  • Direct support to build a working POC or MVP

Intended Audience

  • Business Leaders
  • Functional Experts, Industry SMEs
  • Innovation Teams, Enterprise Architects
  • Technical & Domain Experts (e.g., AI/data-science, Data, CRM, Integration), Sysadmins, Developers

Engagement Timeline

1/2 day to 2+ weeks

Location
