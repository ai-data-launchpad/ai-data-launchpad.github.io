About the AI Data Launchpad Program

AI Data Launchpad is an end-to-end structured program designed to guide your journey from learning to executing within your organization.
  • Instructor-led, hands-on workshop on advanced technologies (e.g., multi-agent autonomous systems)
  • Use-case ideation to identify practical AI applications
  • Direct support to build a working POC or MVP
