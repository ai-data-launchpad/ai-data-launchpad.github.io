About the Workshop

AI Data Launchpad is a full-cycle program designed to guide your journey from learning to executing within your organization. It combines an instructor-led, hands-on workshop on advanced technologies — (e.g., multi-agent autonomous systems) — with use-case ideation to identify practical AI use cases. Participants also receive direct support to build a POC or MVP.
